# The Last Log — Movie Bible

Stress-test fixture for tugboat sandbox. Not a production screenplay.

## Logline

A retired lighthouse keeper finds her dead sister's AI chat logs and must decide whether to publish them before the platform deletes the account.

## Theme

**Stated question (Beat 2):** "Is a digital ghost still a person?"

## Setting

Coastal Maine, present day. Harbor Point Lighthouse (decommissioned, museum-adjacent).

## Characters

| Name | Role |
|------|------|
| **Elena Voss** | Protagonist, 62, retired lighthouse keeper |
| **Clara Voss** | Deceased sister; presence via AI chat logs |
| **Maya Chen** | B-story — local journalist, Elena's niece |
| **Platform counsel** | Antagonist force (Act II) |

## Beat structure

Fifteen beats follow [Save the Cat](https://gist.github.com/dideler/6043168). See [`beat-sheet.md`](beat-sheet.md) and [`../manifests/beats.yaml`](../manifests/beats.yaml) for slugs and grep keywords.
