## Beat 15: Final Image

EXT. LIGHTHOUSE CLIFF - DAWN

The beacon light cuts through the morning mist, its steady rhythm a constant against the shifting tides. Our protagonist, weathered but resolute, stands at the rail, watching the light sweep across the horizon. The journey has been arduous, marked by loss and revelation, yet here, at the edge of the known world, there is a quiet acceptance.

Seagulls wheel overhead, their cries echoing the resolve that has carried them this far. The waves below crash with relentless energy, each surge a reminder of time’s passage. Yet the light persists, unwavering, casting its golden fingers over the water, over the rocks, over the fragile boat tied to the dock—a vessel that once carried fear, now carries hope.

In the distance, the first true sunrise breaks, painting the sky in hues of amber and rose. The protagonist turns from the rail, steps back toward the lighthouse door, and pauses. They glance back one last time at the beacon light, understanding now that it was never just a warning for ships, but a promise: that even in the darkest nights, a steady guide remains.

Fade out as the light continues its eternal sweep, a silent sentinel guarding the shore.
