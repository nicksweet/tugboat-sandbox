## Beat 6: Break into Act II

INT. SECURE FACILITY - SERVER ROOM - NIGHT

Rain streaks the skylight above as JACKIE slips through the ventilation shaft, dropping silently onto the cold floor. Rows of blinking servers hum like a hive. She pulls a rugged tablet from her pack, connects to the mainframe via a fiber-optic tap, and initiates the breach. The screen flashes: ACCESS GRANTED.

She navigates past firewalls, her fingers flying. The logs she needs—encrypted flight data from the night the convoy vanished—are buried deep in subsystem Delta-7. A countdown timer appears: 00:02:00 until auto-wipe.

Jackie whispers, "Come on, come on..." and locates the file. She begins to download the logs, the progress bar crawling forward. Sweat beads on her forehead as the timer hits 00:00:30. With a final burst, the transfer completes.

She snaps the tablet shut, just as the emergency lights flicker red. Alarms wail. Jackie melts into the shadows, the downloaded logs now her only leverage—and the point of no return. Act II begins.
