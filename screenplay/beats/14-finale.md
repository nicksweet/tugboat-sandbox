## Beat 14: Finale

INT. TUGBOAT COMMAND CENTER - NIGHT

The room hums with the soft glow of monitors. CAPTAIN REYES stands before the main screen, where the final logs of the mission scroll in real-time. The crew, exhausted but focused, watches as the last data packets stream from the distant probe.

REYES
(voice steady)
All systems nominal. Begin final transmission.

She taps a sequence of keys. The screen flashes: TRANSMITTING LOGS TO HUB. A progress bar creeps forward. The tension eases as each percentage point climbs.

JENKINS
(softly)
We did it. After all the storms, the detours... we're sending home the truth.

Reyes nods, eyes fixed on the bar hitting 100%. A chime sounds. The logs have been sent.

REYES
(publish the logs)
Now we wait for confirmation.

The crew exchanges relieved smiles. Outside the viewport, the tugboat drifts silently among the stars, its mission complete, its story ready to be told.

FADE OUT.
