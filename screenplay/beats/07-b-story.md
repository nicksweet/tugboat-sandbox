## Beat 7: B Story

INT. COFFEE SHOP - DAY

Maya, a bright-eyed graphic designer in her late 20s, sits sketching in a notebook. She looks up as the protagonist, ALEX, enters, looking troubled. Maya waves him over with a warm smile.

MAYA
(cheerful)
Hey, Alex! Rough day?

ALEX
(sighing)
You could say that. I'm stuck on this project and I don't know where to turn.

MAYA
(empathetic)
You know, sometimes the best ideas come when you're not trying so hard. Want to grab a coffee and brainstorm?

Alex hesitates, then nods. As they talk, Maya's creativity and optimism begin to shift Alex's perspective. She shares her own struggles with creative blocks and how she overcame them by seeking inspiration in everyday moments.

Their conversation flows, and Maya suggests looking at the problem from a different angle — perhaps literally, by changing the environment or perspective. Alex starts to see a glimmer of a solution.

MAYA
(encouraging)
Trust your instincts. You've got this.

Alex leaves the coffee shop with renewed energy, inspired by Maya's unexpected wisdom. The B Story of friendship and mentorship begins to unfold, highlighting the importance of human connection in overcoming obstacles.
